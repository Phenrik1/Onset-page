About the Kinship

Lore & Purpose

The Hammer of Aulë is a kinship dedicated to fellowship, craftsmanship, and shared adventure. We walk the halls of Middle‑earth as comrades, not strangers – from the quiet roads of the Shire to the deepest shadows of Moria.

We value steady progress over haste, and honor over glory. Whether you come to forge, to fight, or to follow the stories of the Free Peoples, you will find a place at our hearth.

Server: Sting

Our Home

We dwell on the Sting server – a close‑knit community where names are remembered and kinships endure. Our hall stands open to those who seek a stable, lore‑aware group with a long‑term view.

  • Time zones: EU‑friendly activity
  • Playstyle: Casual‑to‑organized, with room for both new and veteran players
  • Focus: Group content, story, crafting, and social events

Kinship Values

What We Stand For

Our hall is built on a few simple, unshakable stones:

  • Respect for every member, regardless of level or experience
  • Patience and guidance for those learning the game or returning after a break
  • Reliability in group content – if we commit, we show up
  • Enjoyment of the world and its stories, not just the endgame

Roster & Roles

The Company

We welcome a balanced company of Free Peoples:

■ Tanks & Wardens ■ Healers & Support ■ Melee & Ranged DPS ■ Crafters & Gatherers

Whether you bring a single main or a stable of alts, there is room in the hall. We value reliability and attitude more than perfect builds.

Events & Raids

Forged in Battle

We organize regular kinship runs, from fellowship instances to larger raids, depending on interest and availability.

  • Planned group nights announced in advance in Discord
  • Mixture of progression runs and relaxed “for fun” evenings
  • No mandatory attendance – real life comes first

If you enjoy learning encounters together and improving as a group, you will feel at home here.
