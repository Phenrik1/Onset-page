Kinship Charter

We stand together as a company of friends, crafters, and companions. Respect, patience, and reliability are the pillars of our hall. New and returning players are welcome – we walk at the pace of the fellowship, not the fastest runner.

Hall Etiquette

Keep the hall welcoming. No harassment, no spoilers without warning, and no pressure to play a certain way. Real life comes first – always.

Upcoming Runs

Use the #raids-and-runs channel to propose dates and times. Once agreed, an officer can pin the plan and note roles needed.

Suggested Raid Nights

Two evenings per week, EU‑friendly times. We aim for steady progress, not speed clears. If you’re new to group content, this is a safe place to learn.
